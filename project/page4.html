About Dolma

Once again brutal truth takes precedence. Writing four pages on Middle Eastern Food is difficult, but Middle Eastern food is basically synonymous for "Mediterranean Food". In fact, this entire paper is basically because I'm upset that LUNA GRILL has none of these foods. I get why they might not Shawarma (actually I don't), or falafel (ok, they do have falafel, but I couldn't very well NOT mention falafel), and Halva. But Dolma is very clearly Greek and they should have those. Oh, and by the way Dolma is stuffed grape leaves.
